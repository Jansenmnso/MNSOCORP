Final Certified Results – 2025 MNSO Leadership Election

Candidate Name Party Total Votes
Jansen Monk Conservative 512
Jerome Foster II Liberal 447
Janathe Nilsen Labor 62
Jace McMillan Green 29

Election Summary: The MNSO 2025 Election was the first official live broadcasted election in the early history of the Organization. A total of 502 members participated in the vote.

The Conservative Party, represented by Jansen Monk, reached the 500-vote threshold first, securing the election victory. Runner-up Jerome Foster II represented the Liberal Party.

The election was a milestone in MNSO’s democratic development, symbolizing its commitment to youth political participation and transparency.

This election result has been reviewed and certified by the MNSO Election Committee.
Certification Date: May 3, 2025
